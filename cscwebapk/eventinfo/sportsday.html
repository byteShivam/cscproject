Chandrabhan Sharma College Sports Tournament

About the Tournament

CSC is excited to announce its upcoming Sports Day event, which promises to be a fun-filled and action-packed day of competition and camaraderie. This event will bring together students, faculty, and staff from all departments and backgrounds to participate in a range of athletic events, including track and field, basketball, football, volleyball, and more. The goal of this event is to promote fitness, wellness, and sportsmanship among the college community. Participants will compete in various individual and team events, with trophies and prizes awarded to the winners. There will also be food, music, and other entertainment throughout the day to keep everyone energized and engaged. This event promises to be an exciting and memorable occasion, providing an opportunity for everyone to come together, have fun, and showcase their sporting talents.
