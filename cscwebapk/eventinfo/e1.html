Chandrabhan Sharma College E-Sports Tournament

About the Tournament

Chandrabhan Sharma College is excited to announce its upcoming e-sports tournament featuring popular mobile games such as PUBG Mobile, COD, and Freefire. The tournament aims to provide a platform for gaming enthusiasts to showcase their skills and compete with each other in a friendly and exciting environment. To participate in the tournament, participants must be currently enrolled in the college and have a valid mobile device to play the games. Interested students can form teams of up to four members and register for the tournament through the official college website. The tournament will be held over a period of two weeks, with the top teams from each game advancing to the final round. The winners will receive exciting prizes, including cash rewards, gaming gear, and certificates of appreciation.

Games

  • PUBG Mobile
  • COD
  • Freefire

Each game will have its own set of rules and regulations, which will be announced prior to the start of the tournament.
